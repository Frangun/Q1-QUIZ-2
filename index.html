Howl's Moving Castle

Play this as you read my page :)



This movie/film is animated by Studio Ghibli , one of the most popular studios among anime, and is distributed by Toho.

Sophie, a young milliner destined to be with Howl, is casted an unexpected curse from the Witch of the Waste.
Due to her appearance, she is left with no choice but to hide from her family and seek magic that could return her back to normal.






Character Details


  1. Sophie:Protagonist, Howl's lover, spreads her kindness throughout the story
  2. Howl: Protagonist, Sophie's lover, cursed wizard
  3. Witch of the Waste: Antagonist, Howl's ex lover
